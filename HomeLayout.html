Welcome to the Reservation portal of the Indian Government. This portal has been developed to determine and analyse the benefits the reserved and backward classes of our country need. The major backward classes are the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled tribes.
Other Backward Classes include the Nomadic tribes class(I, II, III) and Tribal individuals.
India is a secular and sovereign country. Hence by providing just sufficient benefits to a particular classes of people may help them grow shoulder to shoulder in the society.

The primary objective of the present-day Indian reservation system is to increase the opportunities for enhancing the social and educational status of underprivileged communities and thus improve their lives so they can have their place in the mainstream of Indian society.
